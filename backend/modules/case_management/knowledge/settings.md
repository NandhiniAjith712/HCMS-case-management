# Settings

HCMS settings control the behavior of the case management system. Most settings are managed by administrators or HR managers.

## What employees can see

Employees usually do not access system settings. Their experience is controlled by the organization-wide configuration.

## What administrators configure

- Departments and subcategories
- SLA response and resolution times
- Escalation levels and assignments
- Notification preferences
- Special case permissions
- Routing rules

If an employee has a question about why the system behaves a certain way, the answer is usually because of these configured settings. Employees should not change settings themselves.
