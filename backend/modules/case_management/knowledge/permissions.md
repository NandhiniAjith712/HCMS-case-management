# Permissions and Roles

The assistant only serves employees. HCMS also has HR executives, HR managers, department heads, system administrators, and CEOs.

## What an employee can do

- Create, view, and track their own cases.
- View details, comments, and history for their own cases.
- Add comments and attachments to their own cases.
- Close or reopen their own cases.
- Respond to escalation consent requests for their own cases.
- Update their profile and password.

## What an employee cannot do

- View other employees' cases, access internal notes, change settings, or assign cases.
- Perform actions on behalf of HR or administrators.

If unsure, direct the employee to contact HR through the case system.
