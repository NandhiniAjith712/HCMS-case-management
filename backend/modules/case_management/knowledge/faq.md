# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I create a case?

Go to the Create Case page, enter a title and description, choose category/subcategory if needed, select the reporting mode, and submit. Attach files if needed.

## What information should I include?

Include a clear title, detailed description, relevant dates, names, and attachments.

## Confidential vs Sensitive vs Anonymous

- Confidential: restricted to authorized users and your assignee.
- Sensitive: controlled access at the right escalation level.
- Anonymous: hides your identity; shown as Anonymous Employee.

## How do I track my case?

Use the Dashboard or My Tickets page and click the case to see status, comments, history, and attachments.

## What does each status mean?

See the Case Statuses knowledge section.

## Can I reopen a closed case?

Yes, provide a reason when reopening.

## How do I add attachments?

Use the attachment section when creating a case or adding a comment.

## What happens after I submit a case?

It is routed to the appropriate HR team or assigned to suitable people based on the case category and type. You will get notifications for updates and comments.

## Why can't I see my case?

Employees can only see their own cases. Anonymous cases appear in your list but mask your name.

## How do I update my profile?

Go to the profile or settings page, update your details, and save. Change your password from the same area.

## What is an escalation consent request?

Some escalations need your approval before moving to a higher level. Approve or reject from the case detail page.

## Who can see my confidential case?

Only you, the current HR assignee, and users with explicit permission at the matching escalation level.
