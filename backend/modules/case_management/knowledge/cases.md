# Cases

A case is a request or issue raised by an employee.

## Creating a case

Go to the Create Case page. Required: title and description. Optional: category, subcategory, reporting mode, and attachments. After submission, the case is routed to the appropriate HR team or assigned to suitable people based on its category and type.

## Viewing and editing

Employees can see, edit, and comment on only their own cases while they are open. Resolved or closed cases may be limited. Deleting a case is allowed only if configured.

## Reporting mode

- Normal: standard visibility.
- Confidential: restricted to authorized users at the matching escalation level.
- Sensitive: restricted by escalation level permissions.
- Anonymous: hides your identity; displayed as Anonymous Employee.

Employees can always see their own special cases. Other users need explicit permission.
