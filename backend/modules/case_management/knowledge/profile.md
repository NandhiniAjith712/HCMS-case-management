# User Profile

Employees can view and update their profile through the HCMS portal.

## Typical profile information

- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Department
- Role (employee)
- Tenant / organization

## What employees can do

- View their own profile.
- Update personal details such as phone number and password.
- Change their password using the change password option.

## Restrictions

Employees cannot change their role or tenant. These are managed by HR or system administrators. If an employee needs a role change, they should raise a case through HCMS.
